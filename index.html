Campus Trackis an AI-based face recognition system designed to enhance campus security and streamline attendance tracking. Time-Series Modeling of Negative Price Duration & Recovery

Technologies: Python, LSTM, SARIMA, XGBoost, Pandas, Matplotlib

We developed a predictive framework to analyze negative price events and recovery in European energy markets, using SARIMA, LSTM, and XGBoost, with a focus on Denmark due to its high occurrence of negative prices. The framework enhanced model performance by engineering time-based and price-based features like COSINE&SINE hour. The LSTM model achieved the best forecasting accuracy (R²: 0.92, RMSE: 16.72) and identified key factors influencing negative prices, such as weekdays, renewable energy availability, and cyclical patterns.
